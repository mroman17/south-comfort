Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical AC repair take? expand_more

Most common repairs, such as capacitor replacements or refrigerant recharges, are completed within 1 to 2 hours. More complex issues involving motors or compressors may take 3 to 4 hours. Our technicians arrive with fully-stocked trucks to ensure most repairs are finished on the first visit.

Do you offer emergency after-hours service? expand_more

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for critical failures. Members of our Pro-Care program receive priority dispatch with no additional emergency fees. For non-emergencies, we typically offer same or next-day scheduling.

Is my old AC unit worth repairing or should I replace? expand_more

We follow the "Rule of 5,000" — multiply the age of the unit by the repair cost. If it exceeds $5,000, we typically recommend a more energy-efficient replacement. However, we always provide an honest assessment of your unit's current condition and expected lifespan.
